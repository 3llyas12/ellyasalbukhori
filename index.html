Duke of Edinburgh Award

Duke of Edinburgh Award is an international award commonly known as Anugerah Remaja Perdana in Malaysia. Throughout my high school years, I have managed to obtain the bronze and silver award respectively. The award is achieved by completing required hours of activities from 4 categories.
